LISTS OF TOUR

JAISALMER

Jaisalmer, Rajasthan: Known for its magnificent forts and golden sand dunes. Key attractions include Jaisalmer Fort and Sam Sand Dunes. Read more

UDAIPUR

Udaipur, Rajasthan: Known as the "City of Lakes," Udaipur is famous for its beautiful lakes, palaces, and traditional architecture. Key attractions include Lake Pichola, City Palace, and Jag Mandir. Read more

MUSSOORIE

Mussoorie, Uttarakhand: Mussoorie is a picturesque hill station nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas. It offers breathtaking views, pleasant weather, and attractions like Gun Hill and Kempty Falls. Read more

MOUNT ABU

Mount Abu, Rajasthan: The only hill station in Rajasthan, Mount Abu is famous for its serene beauty and attractions like Dilwara Jain Temples and Nakki Lake. Read more

SHIMLA

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh: Shimla is a popular hill station known for its colonial charm and snow-capped mountains. Visitors can explore attractions like The Mall Road and Jakhu Temple. Read more

GOA

Goa, India: Goa is a beach destination famous for its vibrant nightlife, sandy beaches, and Portuguese heritage. Key attractions include Baga Beach, Fort Aguada, and Basilica of Bom Jesus. Read more

MANALI

Manali, Himachal Pradesh: Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, Manali is a popular hill station offering adventure activities like trekking and skiing. Key attractions include Rohtang Pass and Hadimba Temple. Read more

GULMARG

Gulmarg, Jammu and Kashmir: Gulmarg is a popular skiing destination with stunning landscapes and snow-covered peaks. Visitors can enjoy activities like cable car rides and snowboarding. Read more

AGRA

Agra, Uttar Pradesh: Agra is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visitors can explore other attractions like Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri. Read more

VARANASI

Varanasi: It is steeped in history and boasts a rich cultural heritage. The city has been a center of learning, art, music, and literature for centuries. Traditional music, dance, and folk arts thrive in Varanasi. Read more
